PARTIAL WEDDING PLANNING

Have you already planned some aspects of your wedding but want some help and guidance to stay on track? Then the Partial Wedding Planning is the service for you!

You can contact us at any time during the planning of your wedding.

At the initial consultation we can establish what has been planned so far and what help and guidance we can give you with the remaining aspects.

Having a large network of suppliers spanning all over Ireland we can recommend suppliers based on your vision and budget and can arrange all necessary meetings on your behalf which we will also attend. This enables us to negotiate on prices and discounts on your behalf.

If you have already booked supplier we will contact them, with your permission, to arrange all timings and organization for the big day itself.

The team will also be there on the day itself from start to finish to ensure everything runs smoothly and you are able to relax and enjoy your day.

In the final weeks before the big day things start to really step up a gear. Suppliers ask for all the final decisions, final payments and all the small little outstanding tasks become pretty urgent. This is where The Finishing Touches can step in and take away the stress and headaches and allow you to enjoy the build-up to your big day.

We will sit down with you and go through all the plans and bookings you’ve made thus far and we’ll pull everything together even the smallest little detail, coordinating every element. We will ensure your plans are flawless and with our experience we can fill any gaps that may have been overlooked.

Whether you involve us 6 months or 6 weeks prior to your wedding we will include:

  • Full review of your plans to date
  • Final site visit
  • Unlimited contact right up to and including the big day including unlimited calls, Zoom Meets and Emails
  • Supplier logistics plan including any final payments
  • Completion of any outstanding wedding admin

The list is endless – we will do whatever necessary to ensure your day, and the build-up, is flawless and stress free.

For more information or to book a no obligation consultation, in person or via zoom, contact us today via info@thefinishingtouchesni.com
