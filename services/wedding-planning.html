Full Wedding Planning

Do you and your beloved want to sit back, relax and enjoy your big day without having to worry about anything? Then this is the service for you. The team at The Finishing Touches will guide you from the start of your planning right through to the big day itself.

It all starts with an initial consultation where we will really get to know your ideas and dreams for yourwedding including the style/theme that you may have in mind. We will then use our experience, contacts, and planning skills to put all of this together to create the wedding of your dreams.

You will remain in control every step of the way.

We will only select the best suppliers that we feel are right for your wedding.

Using The Finishing Touches means you will get the best price and deals to be had.
